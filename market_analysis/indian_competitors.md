# Indian & South Asian Astrology Competitors

**Date:** February 2026
**Scope:** Major Indian astrology platforms (Vedic / Jyotish), primarily targeting the Indian domestic market and South Asian diaspora

---

## Market Context

India represents one of the largest and most deeply embedded astrology markets in the world:

- **Cultural integration:** Vedic astrology (Jyotish) is woven into everyday Indian life — birth charts (Kundli) are drawn for newborns, consulted for marriage matching, business decisions, and major life events
- **Digital penetration:** India has 700M+ smartphone users; astrology apps among the top 10 most-downloaded utility apps in India (App Annie 2024)
- **Revenue scale:** Astrotalk alone reported $100M+ in annual revenue by 2023, demonstrating the enormous monetization potential
- **Diaspora opportunity:** 32M+ South Asians in the US, UK, Canada, and Australia represent a premium underserved segment that wants Vedic tools with modern UX

## Key Indian Competitors

### 1. Astrotalk

- **URL:** https://astrotalk.com
- **Founded:** 2017 (Puneet Gupta, Gurgaon, India)
- **Type:** Mobile app (iOS/Android) + web
- **Positioning:** India's #1 astrology marketplace — human astrologer consultations via chat, call, and video
- **Revenue:** $100M+ annually (2023); one of the highest-revenue astrology companies globally
- **Downloads:** 50M+ (as of 2024)
- **Monthly Active Users:** 15M+
- **Funding:** Series A & beyond; profitable

**Key Products:**
- **Live astrologer consultation** — 15,000+ verified astrologers on the platform; per-minute billing (₹10–₹200/min)
- **Free Kundli** — Full Vedic birth chart with Lagna chart, Navamsa D9, Dashamsa D10
- **Horoscope Today** — Daily sun-sign and Vedic horoscope
- **Numerology, Tarot, Vastu** — Diversified spiritual services
- **Panchang** — Daily Vedic almanac (muhurta, tithi, nakshatra)
- **Match Making** — Kundli Gun Milan for marriage compatibility (36-point system)
- **AI Astrologer** — ChatGPT-powered preliminary readings before human handoff

**Strengths:**
- Largest verified astrologer marketplace in the world — quality control and reviews create trust
- Network effects: more astrologers → better selection → more users → more astrologers
- Per-minute billing lowers entry barrier (users can try for ₹10–50 before committing)
- Strong SEO dominance for all Vedic astrology keywords in India
- Android-first product aligned with India's dominant mobile OS

**Weaknesses:**
- UI/UX is functional but not modern; lags far behind Co-Star or Pattern aesthetically
- Overwhelmingly India-focused; poor English-language UX for diaspora
- Astrologer quality is variable despite verification process
- No serious Western astrology tools; Tropical zodiac is absent
- Limited AI depth; consultation model depends on human supply

**ARPU:** ~$30/yr (primarily from consultation minutes)

### 2. AstroSage

- **URL:** https://www.astrosage.com
- **Founded:** ~2008 (Punit Pandey)
- **Type:** Web + mobile app (iOS/Android)
- **Positioning:** Comprehensive Vedic astrology platform — free tools + astrologer marketplace
- **Monthly Traffic:** ~12M unique visitors/month (one of the highest in the Vedic space)

**Key Products:**
- **Free Kundli** — Detailed Vedic birth chart with all divisional charts (D1–D60)
- **Vedic Match Making** — Kundli matching with full Ashtakoot analysis and Mangal Dosha check
- **Horoscope** — Daily, weekly, monthly, yearly (sun sign and Vedic moon sign / Rashi)
- **Panchang** — Daily Vedic almanac; highly detailed
- **Numerology** — Pythagorean and Chaldean systems
- **Astrologer Directory** — Paid consultations with listed practitioners
- **AstroSage Varta** — Chat with astrologers (compete with Astrotalk)
- **Lal Kitab** — Lal Kitab remedies calculator (unique feature rare on other platforms)

**Strengths:**
- Deepest free Vedic tool set — divisional charts up to D60, Vimshottari Dasha tree, Ashtakavarga
- Strong SEO; ranks #1 for many Vedic astrology searches globally
- Lal Kitab calculator is a unique differentiator (almost no other platform offers it)
- Trusted by both casual users and serious Jyotish students

**Weaknesses:**
- Very dated UI/UX — web design feels 2008-era; mobile app lags similarly
- Ad-heavy monetization; free tier cluttered with ads and upsells
- Astrologer marketplace less polished than Astrotalk
- English writing quality is often poor (primary audience is Hindi-speaking)
- No Western astrology tools

**ARPU:** ~$5–8/yr (primarily ads + occasional consultation)

### 3. mPanchang

- **URL:** https://www.mpanchang.com
- **Founded:** ~2012
- **Type:** Web + mobile app (iOS/Android)
- **Positioning:** India's most comprehensive daily Panchang (Vedic almanac) + Kundli service
- **Monthly Traffic:** ~8M unique visitors/month

**Key Products:**
- **Daily Panchang** — Complete Vedic almanac: Tithi, Vara, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana, Rahu Kaal, Abhijit Muhurta
- **Muhurta Calculator** — Auspicious timings for weddings, travel, property purchase, business inauguration
- **Kundli** — Vedic birth chart with Lagna, Navamsa, Dasha timeline
- **Kundli Matching** — Gun Milan / Ashtakoot with detailed compatibility report
- **Festival Calendar** — Hindu festivals, tithi-based events, ekadashi dates
- **Shubh Tithi** — Auspicious date finder for events

**Unique Position:**
- The go-to resource for daily Panchang in India — used by millions for muhurta decisions
- Covers regional variants (North Indian, South Indian, Gujarati, Bengali Panchang differences)
- Deeply integrated with Hindu religious calendar — content no Western app touches

**Weaknesses:**
- Niche focus (Panchang-first) limits broader astrology engagement
- Dated mobile UX; primarily a reference tool, not an engaging product
- Limited AI or personalization beyond chart calculation

**ARPU:** ~$2/yr (ads + occasional premium muhurta report)

### 4. Clickastro (Astro-Vision Futuretech)

- **URL:** https://www.clickastro.com
- **Founded:** 1984 (Astro-Vision Futuretech, Kerala, India) — one of the oldest Vedic software companies
- **Type:** Web + mobile app + desktop software
- **Positioning:** Professional Vedic astrology software + consumer reports; trusted by practitioners

**Key Products:**
- **Life Horoscope Report** — Comprehensive 30–100+ page Vedic report (₹499–₹999)
- **Kundli Software** — Desktop professional tool used by astrologers
- **Marriage Matching** — Premium matching report with detailed analysis
- **Numerology & Vastu** — Additional spiritual product lines
- **Jathakam** — South Indian style horoscope (Malayalam/Tamil audience)

**Strengths:**
- 40+ years in Vedic software — deeply trusted by practicing astrologers and older demographics
- South Indian market strength (Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh) where Vedic is most formal
- High-quality report generation; used as reference by professional astrologers
- Supports multiple regional scripts (Malayalam, Tamil, Kannada, Telugu, Hindi output)

**Weaknesses:**
- No modern mobile-first UX; product designed for desktop-era workflows
- Limited social or engagement features
- Brand is not recognized by diaspora or younger Indian-Americans
- No Western astrology

**ARPU:** ~$15/yr (per-report transactional model)

### 5. Cosmic Insights

- **URL:** https://www.cosmicinsights.net / mobile app
- **Founded:** ~2016 (Achala Sylvia, based in US; India-origin)
- **Type:** Mobile app (iOS/Android) + web
- **Positioning:** Modern Vedic astrology for the diaspora and global English-speaking audience; beautifully designed
- **Revenue:** Subscription ~$9.99/month or $49.99/year

**Key Products:**
- **Vedic Birth Chart** — Clean, modern chart wheel (North Indian and South Indian styles)
- **Dasha Timeline** — Vimshottari Dasha, Chara Dasha; visual timeline
- **Transit Tracker** — Vedic transits with interpretations
- **Nakshatra Analysis** — In-depth 27 Nakshatra profiles with remedies
- **Ashtakavarga** — Full Ashtakavarga with Sarvashtakavarga analysis
- **Annual Forecast** — Varshphal (Solar Return equivalent in Vedic)

**Strengths:**
- Best UX/UI in the Vedic app space — clean, modern, feels like a Western wellness app
- Written in clear English — accessible to diaspora and Western practitioners learning Vedic
- Excellent Nakshatra content; deep Jyotish scholarship behind the text
- Growing following among English-speaking Vedic enthusiasts in US/UK/Australia

**Weaknesses:**
- Small team; slower feature development than larger players
- Limited social features
- Premium price point ($49.99/yr) may limit casual conversion
- Mostly ignored by the Indian domestic market (seen as diaspora-focused)

**ARPU:** ~$40/yr

### 6. Guruji Astro

- **URL:** https://gurujiastro.com
- **Type:** Mobile app (iOS/Android)
- **Positioning:** AI-powered Vedic astrology assistant for India's young digital natives
- **Founded:** ~2019 (India)

**Key Products:**
- **AI Kundli Analysis** — Conversational AI that interprets birth chart placements
- **Daily Predictions** — Personalized daily forecast based on Dasha + transits
- **Ask Guruji** — ChatGPT-style conversational Vedic astrology Q&A
- **Remedies** — Gemstone, mantra, and ritual recommendations based on chart

**Strengths:**
- AI-native product; first major Indian platform to deploy LLM astrology chat
- Simple onboarding; targets young urban Indians comfortable with AI assistants
- Covers remedies (gemstones, mantras) which are very high-value in Indian astrology

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller scale vs. Astrotalk and AstroSage
- AI quality inconsistent; lacks depth of human astrologer consultation
- Limited tool breadth (no panchang, no kundli matching yet)

### 7. Bejan Daruwalla / GaneshaSpeaks

- **URL:** https://www.ganeshaspeaks.com
- **Type:** Web + mobile (iOS/Android)
- **Positioning:** Celebrity astrologer brand (Bejan Daruwalla) + Sun-sign content for English-speaking Indians
- **Monthly Traffic:** ~5M/month

**Key Products:**
- Daily/Weekly/Monthly sun sign horoscopes (English)
- Annual horoscope book (bestseller in India)
- Live astrologer chat (premium)
- Numerology and tarot

**Strengths:**
- Celebrity astrologer credibility (Bejan Daruwalla was India's most famous English-language astrologer)
- Strong English-language content targets India's urban/educated class and diaspora
- Annual horoscope book strong physical product revenue stream

**Weaknesses:**
- Brand tied to a deceased founder (Bejan Daruwalla died 2020); brand continuity risk
- Sun-sign focused, not personalized Vedic
- UI/UX dated

## Opportunity Summary for AstroAppV2

AstroAppV2 is uniquely positioned to capture the **South Asian diaspora** segment — 32M+ people who:
- Are comfortable with English-first products (Co-Star, Pattern)
- Want authentic Vedic calculations (not just "sidereal zodiac" as a checkbox)
- Are frustrated by the dated UX of AstroSage and mPanchang
- Are not well-served by Cosmic Insights (limited social, limited Panchang)

**Recommended Vedic features for AstroAppV2 MVP:**

1. Vedic/Lahiri birth chart alongside Tropical (tradition toggle)
2. Vimshottari Dasha timeline (essential; used daily by every Vedic practitioner)
3. Navamsa (D9) chart (highest-request divisional chart)
4. Kundli Matching / Ashtakoot (high-value feature; marriage use case drives engagement)
5. Basic Panchang (daily Tithi, Nakshatra, Rahu Kaal — minimum viable Panchang)
6. Nakshatra profile for Moon + Ascendant (high-engagement content; shareable)
